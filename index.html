About

English Profile

Kohei Yamamoto is an Enablement Lead (commonly Outbound PM), a function within OpenAI HQ Global Team, responsible for AI enablement in the Japan market since 2025. He joined Microsoft as a new graduate in 2021, where he served in various roles, including engineer, evangelist, and architect X, reporting directly to an executive officer and driving cutting-edge initiatives for flagship projects. Concurrently, he was an external research fellow at the National University of Singapore, focusing on spatiotemporal intelligence (Quantitative Human Dynamics). As a Ph.D. student at The University of Tokyo, he researches Cognitive Informatics (Qualitative Human Dynamics). During his studies in the past, he also gained experience as a student fellow at institutions including IBM Research, and he continues to publish papers in IEEE and international journals.
