Who I Am

I am Kohei YAMAMOTO (山本 晃平), Japanese. I first touched Computer Science of the physical layer (CPU architecture, binary programming etc.). However, I have been devoting myself to research areas such as Location Science and Human Computer Interaction during my Bachelor's.

After completing my Bachelor's, I took a gap year for 1.5 years and engaged myself in research/services/products at several IT companies.

Currently, I am pursuing my curiosity in Applied Geographic Information Systems along with Spatiotemporal Modelling, Info. Visualisation, and so on.
